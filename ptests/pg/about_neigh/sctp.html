• Swap and Compact Two Projects (SCTP)

  • The neighborhood purpose is to relocate a sequence based on jobs belonging to two projects p1 and p2, furthermore jobs of p1 and p2 will be compacted, the swap occurs, when the project that finishes last is allocated first. To perform this process, it is necessary to receive as a parameter the allocations sequence S and the projects p1 and p2 .
         At first step, all positions in S are accessed in reverse order to identify between p1 and p2 which finishing last and what are the early indexes allocations of each project. During this process, jobs of each projects p1 and p2 are stored, respectively, in vectors M1 and M2.
         The second step, comprises the swap of sequence, for this it is needed to set a benchmark idxCtrl in S, representing the first job index between the projects p1 and p2. So, all jobs not belonging to projects p1 and p2 and with smaller indexes than idxCrtl are stored in vector L and those with bigger indexes than idxCrtl are stored in vector R.
         After completing the second step, it is necessary unite all jobs contained in M1, M2, L and R. Initially the sequence is rebuilt including in the first place all jobs of L, representing jobs allocated before idxCrtl and not belonging to projects p1 and p2. Soon after, are added in S project jobs that finishing by last (M1 or M2), then jobs project that finishing first (M1 or M2). Finally, all jobs of R are added, representing jobs not belonging to projects p1 and p2 allocated after idxCrtl.

• Compact Project on Extreme (CPE)

    Neighborhood

  • The neighborhood purpose is to compact or compress, on extreme, a project in relation to some position of project’s job. To perform this process, it is necessary to receive as a parameter the allocations sequence S and an index i ∈ S representing the position of job allocation j.
         The project to be compacted, is represented by project pj in which job j belongs. In the first instance, all positions in S are accessed to identify the indexes of the first and last job of pj allocated in S. The indexes of these jobs are stored, respectively, in idxStart and idxEnd.
         After setting indexes, it’s possible compress all jobs in pj in relative to the position i ∈ S of job j. The procedure to compact the project it is simple, just shifting to the right all jobs s ∈ S, pj where idxStart < s < idxEnd and to the left where i < s < idxEnd.

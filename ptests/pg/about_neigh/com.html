  • This neighborhood includes a simple movement, swap one execution mode m of a particular job j. This neighborhood verifies if the changes of modes are valid or not in relation to non-renewable resources.
         To perform this process, it is necessary to receive as a parameter a vector M of execution modes for all jobs, the job j and a new mode m.
         Figure 11 shows the movement made by this neighborhood. In this example, the method receives as parameter job 1 and a new mode 0. Thus, the current execution mode 1 of this job is changed to 0.
